1. Draw a horizontal line on the paper. It should be roughly in the middle.

2. Put the point of the compass on the line (somewhere near the middle, we'll call it point A) and draw a circle.

3. Make the compasses wider (about twice as wide), then place the point of the compasses on one of the points where the line crosses the circle (point B). Draw an arc (a part of a circle) below the line.
